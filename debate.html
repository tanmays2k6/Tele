About Eloquence

Established in 2015, our debate club has a rich history of fostering intellectual growth and oratorical excellence among generations of students.

Our Mission

At Eloquence, we strive to create a community where students can develop critical thinking skills, gain confidence in public speaking, and engage with complex societal issues through structured debate.

A dynamic debate format that emulates the British parliamentary system, emphasizing impromptu speaking and quick thinking.
