About Brainwave

Established in 2010, our quiz club has been promoting intellectual curiosity and the joy of learning across a wide range of subjects.

Our Mission

At Brainwave, we aim to create a vibrant community where students can enhance their knowledge, develop quick thinking abilities, and engage in friendly intellectual competition while building connections across disciplines.

Our Achievements

13+ Years of Excellence

Rapid Fire Quiz

A fast-paced format testing quick recall with time-bound rounds, challenging participants to think on their feet with minimal hesitation.

Themed Quizzes

Specialized rounds focused on particular subjects from pop culture and sports to science and history, allowing participants to showcase their expertise.

Team Challenge

Collaborative quizzing where teams of 3-4 members pool their knowledge across diverse topics to solve complex questions and puzzles.
