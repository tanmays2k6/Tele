Our Story

Campus Connect began as a spontaneous idea during a web development hackathon. What started as a simple project to streamline student interaction quickly evolved into something bigger. Fueled by the passion and creativity of a few college friends, the platform grew beyond expectations. Today, Campus Connect stands as one of the leading platforms for enhancing campus life. It bridges the gap between students and their college by centralizing events, clubs, announcements, and resources—all in one place. Whether you're looking to join a new club, find out what’s happening this week, or connect with like-minded peers, Campus Connect makes it seamless. Built by students, for students, Campus Connect continues to evolve as a vibrant digital campus community.
